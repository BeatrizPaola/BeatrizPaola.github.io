Influence of climate on our health

We have all tastes and flavors, some people prefer the cold rather than the heat, sunny, windy, rainy or cloudy, the truth is that spending time outdoors is good for all of us. Did you know that exposure to the sun brings us many benefits, some of them are...? It helps the body for the generation of vitamin D, helps prevent and control acne, gives us more defenses for our body, improves our mood as it promotes the synthesis of serotonin, a substance related to well-being, these are some of the benefits that the sun brings us, but not everything is perfect, it is good to protect our skin from ultraviolet rays and avoid constant and indiscriminate exposure to the sun. Remember to always use sunscreen and a hat. Remember everything in excess is bad. Have a great day!
